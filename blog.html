The Complete Guide to Child Trafficking Warning Signs Every Parent Should Know

Learn to identify behavioral, physical, and relational warning signs of trafficking. This comprehensive guide helps parents, teachers, and community members recognize when a teenager is at risk. We'll cover the subtle changes in behavior that often indicate a child is being exploited, including withdrawal from family activities, unexplained financial resources, new relationships with older individuals, and changes in school attendance or grades. Understanding these warning signs can be lifesaving, as early intervention is critical in preventing trafficking situations from escalating. Our research team has compiled the most up-to-date information from verified survivors, child protection experts, and law enforcement agencies to create this definitive resource for anyone who works with or cares about teenagers.
