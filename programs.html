Core Initiatives

Our
Programs.

Four integrated programs protecting teenagers and preventing trafficking across all 64 districts of Bangladesh.

Flagship Program

Youth Protection Network

TeenX's flagship program establishes trained youth protection coordinators (ages 16-35) as community protection leaders in all 64 districts of Bangladesh. Youth Envoys receive three weeks of comprehensive training covering trafficking indicators, psychological first aid, community organizing, and rapid response protocols.

  • ✓ 1,000+ trained Youth Envoys
  • ✓ 240+ trafficking cases prevented/responded in 2025
  • ✓ 45 minutes average emergency response time
Education

Anti-Trafficking Education

Comprehensive education program reaching 100,000+ teenagers annually through school-based curricula, community awareness campaigns, digital safety training, and occupational sector programs. Works with 500+ schools and conducts 50+ large-scale awareness campaigns reaching 5+ million people.

  • ✓ 100,000+ teenagers trained annually
  • ✓ 500+ schools partnered
  • ✓ 92% awareness improvement rate
Awareness

Community Awareness Campaigns

Large-scale national campaigns like #Protected reach 5+ million Bangladeshis through digital platforms, radio, SMS, printed materials, school assemblies, and community events. Aims to normalize trafficking prevention discussions, reduce survivor stigma, and mobilize community-wide protection networks.

  • ✓ 5+ million people reached annually
  • ✓ 500,000+ families engaged
  • ✓ Multi-platform approach (digital, radio, SMS, print)
Support

Survivor Rehabilitation & Support

Comprehensive support for trafficking survivors including vocational training, economic rehabilitation, mental health counseling, legal assistance, and family reunification support. Ensures zero re-trafficking through economic empowerment and psychological healing.

  • ✓ 1,200+ survivors served annually
  • ✓ 99% survival rate
  • ✓ 0% re-trafficking rate
