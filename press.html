Government Announces Partnership with TeenX for National Youth Protection Strategy

The Government of Bangladesh has formally partnered with TeenX to integrate the Youth-Led Prevention Model into the National Child Protection Strategy. This marks a significant milestone in scaling youth-based trafficking prevention across all government institutions and represents government validation of our community-based approach.
